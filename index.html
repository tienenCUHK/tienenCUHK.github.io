Bio

I am a PhD student at the Department of Surgery, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biomedical science, with a focus on protein design, generative models, and computational pathology.

I work on developing AI methods for molecular design and biological discovery, including generative diffusion models for nanobody design and multi-modal learning frameworks for disease prediction.

Research Interests:

  • Protein Design — generative models for antibody/nanobody engineering
  • Generative Models — diffusion and flow-based methods for molecular generation
  • Computational Pathology — multi-modal learning for disease analysis
  • AI for Science — bridging machine learning with biological discovery

I am always open to collaborations and discussions. Feel free to reach out if you share similar interests!
